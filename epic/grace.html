Grace The Groupie Persona

Persona

  • Name: Grace the Groupie
  • Gender: Female
  • Age: 23
  • Technology/Devices: Microsoft Surface Laptop, iPhone 10
  • Proficiency: Grace has a lot of experience with social media and using websites like ticketmaster, spotify, and shazam. She is a seasoned traveler and goes to concerts often and wants to plan a trip to follow her favorite band.
  • Do they love or hate it?: Grace loves to use websites and apps that allow her to look at all types of concerts in her area. She is a casual user so as long as she has wifi she typically does not care what device she is on.
  • Attitudes & Needs: Grace loves to camp and will travel anywhere if she gets to see a band that she likes play live. She is very easy going, posts a lot of photos/her location on social media, is single with no children,
  • Why are they using your site?: Grace is using our site to organized her concert schedule, keep tabs on certain musical artists, and be notified about live music happening near her.
  • What need does this persona have that your application aims to fill? Our application will fill her need of organizing a schedule of concerts

User Story

As a casual user, Grace wants to search for concerts she is interested in and never miss when one of her favorite bands is on tour.

Grace finds a band she is interested in, after browsing through the bands upcoming concerts she realizes she wants to stay up to date with their performances. Grace clicks on the favorite icon and the band is added to her follow list. She will now be notified of all future performances by the band.
