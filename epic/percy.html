Persona:Percy the Planner

  • Name: Percy Smith
  • Gender: Male
  • Age: 28
  • Technology: Technically a beginner, he uses IOS Chrome on his Iphone X and Chrome on his 2013 Macbook pro, he just prefers things to work without being complicated.
  • Love or Hate Relationship? Percy is ambivalent towards tech, he wants it to work when he uses it.
  • Needs: Percy is a punctual planner, and needs to have a site where he can plan out his upcoming concerts and have an easy view of where he wants to go, and what bands he wants to see.
  • Why is Percy using our Site? Percy is using our site to provide a place where he can see concerts in his area, as well as schedule them out. He likes to see many venues in one location, and lots of different genres!
  • Needs our Application Fills: Our application aims to fill Percy's need for a calendar and ability to see many venues and bands that are playing.
  • Other Traits: Percy is a man of punctuality, and appreciates seeing as much live music as possible. He likes things to be simple, and work flawlessly, similar to his tech preferences.

User Story

As a power user, Percy wants to create a calendar and see all events local to him, regardless of venue. He wants to add concerts to a personalized calendar, as a logged in user.

Use Case

Percy logs into our website to begin his weekly planning of concerts. After looking through the concerts, he finds some concerts that he is interested in, and wants to add them to his personalized calendar.

Interaction Flow

Pre-Condition: Percy is logged into ConcertCrawl.

Post-Condition: Percy has added a concert to his calendar.

  1. Percy types his zip code into the search bar of the browser.
  2. The browsers saves this information.
  3. Percy selects a date range of this week.
  4. The browser responds by pulling up a list of concerts that are happening this week.
  5. Percy selects a concert that he is interested in seeing!
  6. The browser opens a popup with the info for that concert, such as date, time, and venue.
  7. Percy pushes the add to calendar button.
  8. The browser adds this to Percy's personalized calendar.
